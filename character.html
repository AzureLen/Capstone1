Elesis
  • The Knight
  • Elesis is a Kanavan Knight. She is the daughter of the Captain of the Pursuit Team that tracked Kaze'aze long ago. Having received warrior training from a young age, thanks to her father's position as a Knight, she possesses exceptional skills. When the pursuit team's whereabouts became unknown, Elesis went on a search for her father. Upon learning about Kaze'aze in Serdin, she disguised herself and became part of Serdin's Grand Chase.
Lire
  • The Archer
  • Lire is an elf from Eryuell Island. The Eryuell Island elves are famous for their outstanding fight power, but they stay away from human wars due to their peaceful nature. But the elves formed an investigation Elf Troop upon discovering that Kaze'aze was at the root of the war that spread through the continent in addition to causing many other strange incidents. Lire is a member of the Elf Troop that fights alongside the Grand Chase.
Arme
  • The Mage
  • A magician from the Magical Kingdom, Serdin. A cheerful girl with exceptional skills as a magician. Despite her young age, she became a member of Serdin's greatest magician's guild, the Violet Magician Guild. She has the skill to control both dark and white again, but her curiosity about all magic types does not cease. Knowing Kaze'aze's magicial strength, Arme joined the Grand Chase hoping to meet Kaze'aze.
